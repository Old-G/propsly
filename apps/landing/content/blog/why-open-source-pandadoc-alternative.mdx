## The Problem With Proposal Software Today

If you've ever tried to send a professional proposal, you know the drill. You sign up for PandaDoc or Proposify, build your first document, and then hit the paywall. Want to track views? That's a paid plan. Need e-signatures? Upgrade. Custom branding? Enterprise tier.

The proposal software market has a fundamental problem: **the tools that should empower small businesses end up taxing them**.

Here's what we keep hearing from freelancers and agencies:

- Pricing starts reasonable but scales aggressively with team size
- Templates feel rigid and hard to customize
- Proposals look like PDFs, not modern web experiences
- You're locked into a vendor with no way to export your data
- Self-hosting isn't even an option

> "I just want to send a good-looking proposal without paying $49/month per user. Is that too much to ask?" — Actual feedback from a freelance designer

## Why Open Source Changes Everything

We believe proposal software should be a commodity, not a luxury. That's why Propsly is licensed under AGPL-3.0 and will always be free to self-host.

Open source means:

- **No vendor lock-in.** Your proposals, your data, your server. Export everything at any time.
- **Transparent development.** Every feature, bug fix, and roadmap decision happens in the open on GitHub.
- **Community-driven.** The people who use the tool shape its future, not a sales team optimizing for enterprise upsells.
- **Security you can verify.** Audit the code yourself instead of trusting a black box.

## What Propsly Does Differently

Instead of generating static PDFs, Propsly creates proposals as **interactive web pages**. Your client gets a unique link that loads a fast, mobile-friendly page with real-time pricing tables, embedded media, and built-in e-signatures.

Under the hood, we use a TipTap-based block editor that gives you the flexibility of Notion with the polish of a design tool. Every block is a React component, so extending the editor is as simple as writing a new component.

## The Vision

Our goal is straightforward: build the proposal tool we wish existed. One that's beautiful, fast, open, and free. We're starting with the core editor, tracking, and signatures. From there, we'll add AI-assisted writing, payment collection, and CRM integrations, all driven by what the community actually needs.
